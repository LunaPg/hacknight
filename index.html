We're female developers in Montreal. We decided that Montreal needs another event for female developers to hang out, talk technology, and hack on cool projects.

Bring yourself, your vexing technical problems, and your expertise! If you feel like coding, bring a laptop. We provide food, internet and witty banter.

If you're interested in giving a talk at a future meetup or have any other suggestions, questions, or photos of cats you'd like to share, email us.

FAQ

  • Is there internet?
    Yes! Internet is nerd food and we will have internet for you to hack on your things that need hacking.
  • Is there food?
    We will feed you pizza! There will be vegetarian food but not vegan or gluten-free options. Email us if this is a problem for you and we'll see what we can do in future events.
  • Can I bring kids?
    We don't have any ability to provide childcare, so it's best if you don't. If this is stopping you from coming, let us know!
  • Is the space wheelchair-accessible?
    Yes or no.
